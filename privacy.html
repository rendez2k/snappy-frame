Snappy Frame

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-12

Snappy Frame does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It is a screenshot beautifier that runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no third-party tracking or advertising. We do not have a server that receives your data.

Your images

Images you import and edit are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to us or anyone else. When you export, the image is saved to your device (or shared via your device's own share sheet) — it does not pass through us.

Optional "grab a webpage" feature

If you use the optional feature that captures a webpage from a URL you type, that URL is sent to a third-party screenshot service (such as ScreenshotOne or WordPress mShots) solely to render the page. We do not log it. Any API key you choose to enter is stored only on your own device and is sent only to that service. If you never use this feature, no network request is made.

Data storage

Your settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser/app storage on your device. You can clear them at any time by clearing the app's data.

Children

Snappy Frame does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
